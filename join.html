Join the BizDogs Living Learning Community

Experience the premier residential program for first-year business students at UMD. Connect with ambitious peers, gain exclusive access to professional development opportunities, and build the foundation for your business career.

How to Join

  1. Click BizDogs on your preferred housing on the application
  2. Submit your application and await confirmation
  3. Once accepted, you will receive a welcome email with next steps and resources to get started

Why Join the BizDogs?

Joining BizDogs is an investment in your professional success and personal growth. Here's why our members thrive:

  • Academic & Professional Excellence: Members demonstrate higher engagement and retention rates, supported by evidence-based learning communities that foster success.
  • Exclusive Opportunities: Access sessions and events available only to BizDogs members designed to advance your educational development and establish professional goals.
  • Faculty & Staff Support: Interact with dedicated LSBE faculty and staff committed to helping you navigate your academic journey and achieve your goals.
  • Early Access & Priority: Move into residence halls before others and secure guaranteed seats in required BizDogs classes.
  • Industry Connections: Network with LSBE alumni to gain insight on career paths, success strategies, and real-world professional opportunities.
  • Peer Learning & Support: Attend study groups and collaborative learning sessions right within your BizDogs Community, studying alongside classmates.
  • Diverse Community: Meet students from all over the state, country, and world who bring different backgrounds, perspectives, and ideas to enrich your experience.
  • Shared Academic Interests: Live and study with other students who share similar professional ambitions and academic interests.
  • Lasting Friendships: Develop meaningful friendships with students you'll be taking classes with, creating a supportive network that extends beyond your academic career.
